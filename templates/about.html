Our Mission

In industrial environments, worker safety is a top priority. However, ensuring compliance with safety regulations and identifying potential hazards in real time remains a challenge. We are developing an AI-powered system for monitoring unsafe behavior in factories, integrating safety analysis, data analysis, and predictive modeling to enhance workplace security.

Safety Analysis

Our system continuously monitors worker behavior using computer vision and deep learning models to detect unsafe activities such as improper PPE usage, unsafe lifting techniques, proximity to hazardous machinery, and rule violations. By providing real-time alerts, it enables immediate corrective actions, reducing the likelihood of accidents.

Data Analysis & Insights

collecting and analyzing workplace safety data, our AI-driven system helps organizations identify trends and recurring safety violations. The system generates comprehensive reports that offer data-driven insights into risk factors, enabling proactive safety interventions. These insights help improve training programs, optimize workflows, and ensure regulatory compliance.
